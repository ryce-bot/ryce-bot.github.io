Music

Ever since I was a kid, I was told I was exceptional in the field of music. I mostly blame it on my mom since she would play classical music while she was pregnant with me. I started singing at two and then started playing piano at six. Not only that, I learned how to play the acoustic guitar around 2022 and then switched to an electric guitar earlier this December. I joined the school's choir

and recently got elected as the choirmaster. I've also performed solo at places like weddings, musicals, and school events. I write my own songs and arrange covers and I used to visit this studio a lot with the intent of producing my own songs, although I have been producing some songs at home on my computer. I volunteer in church to lead worship. I take big inspiration from Ariana Grande and Lizzy McAlpine but I also listen to other

genres ranging from classical music, to hip-hop, to metal. I personally love musicals like Hamilton, Dear Evan Hansen, and Wicked and I aspire to someday be part of a broadway musical.

Photography

Aside from music, I also love photography. It started when I wanted to try out for photojournalism but I had no experience. My friend taught me the basics and I found out I was a natural at handling the camera. I started to build my hobby by taking photos of nature and my surroundings. I started with a Fujifilm X-T10 but as I grew in my craft, I moved to an Alpha 6700.
